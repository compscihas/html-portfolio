The Best Movies According to Hasan

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Interstellar

Interstellar is an incredible movie because it brings together mind-blowing visuals and a story that really tugs at your heart. It's not just another sci-fi film; it dives into real science concepts like black holes and time travel, which makes it even more fascinating. The music by Hans Zimmer is unforgettable and adds so much emotion to every scene. Matthew McConaughey's performance is so relatable, balancing all the big space stuff with real human feelings. In the end, it makes you think about big questions like love, survival, and what it means to be human.

Shutter Island

Shutter Island is an intense psychological thriller that keeps you guessing right until the end. The way it mixes mystery, suspense, and drama makes it impossible to look away. Leonardo DiCaprio’s performance is raw and emotional, pulling you into the mind of his troubled character. The eerie atmosphere and stunning cinematography create a sense of unease that builds throughout the film. By the time it's over, you're left questioning reality and the line between sanity and madness, which is what makes it such a powerful movie.

Scarface

Tony Montana, the ultimate rise-and-fall story, is larger than life in Scarface and an absolute blast to watch. He starts from nothing, hustling his way to the top with a "say hello to my little friend" attitude that’s both terrifying and hilarious. Al Pacino nails the role, making Tony a character you weirdly root for, even though he’s totally unhinged. The movie is full of over-the-top moments, wild quotes, and enough swagger to fill an entire Cuban cigar factory.
